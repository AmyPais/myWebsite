What is Hawaii famous for? Hawaii is known for its 750 miles of spotless coastline dotted with volcanoes, its ancient culture that includes icons like the hula dance and lūʻaus, as well as for its rich cuisine that gave us poke bowls. UNIQUE FACTS ABOUT HAWAII Surfing was invented in Hawaii. You can mail a coconut from here. Maui's Mount Haleakala is the largest dormant volcano in the world. Hawaii is the only U.S. state with two official languages.
